Vision

The vision of the Department is to build a strong research and teaching environment pertaining to the broad areas of Computer Science & Engineering in order to meet the challenges of the 21st Century.

Mission

We provide for our students an education in Computer Science & Engineering and attempt to instill in them the attitude & values that will prepare them for lifetime of continued learning & leadership.
Through research & dissemination through scholarly publications, the Department strives to generate new knowledge & technology to serve the professional societies, the community & the nation.
