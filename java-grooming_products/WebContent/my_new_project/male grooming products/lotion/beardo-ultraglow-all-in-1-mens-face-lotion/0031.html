Frequently Asked Questions

Is this cream or facewash?

It is a multipurpose cream not a facewash.

 

Is it a fairness cream?

This lotion is enriched with natural active ingredients which provides protection and moisturization to the skin. It is formulated to be effective on men's skin along with sun screen of SPF30 that blocks UVA/UVB rays and prevents premature ageing of skin. It reduces dark spots, lightens the skin and also controls oil production, firms and improves elasticity of skin giving the skin a healthy radiant look. 

 

How often should it be used?

We recommend to use it before stepping out in the skin or use it daily after shower to moisturize your skin or if you wish to reduce the dark spots, apply it and leave it overnight.


How to use it?

Squeeze the required amount and apply it all over the face and neck with gentle upward strokes. Massage over face and neck with gentle circular motions. For best results use it daily after washing the face with Beardo Active Charcoal Facewash.
